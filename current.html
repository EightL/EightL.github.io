Thesis pitch (unofficial)

Research Goal

Compare co‑operative (α > 0) and competitive (α = 0) reward structures in a team‑versus‑boss scenario.

Arena Environment

  • Discrete 10 × 10 grid built in Gymnasium.
  • Joint inventory & role specialisation (gatherers vs fighters).
  • Final boss spawns centrally; joint kill grants coalition bonus.

Key Metrics

  • time_to_boss_kill — steps until boss defeated
  • num_joint_events — joint gather / attack counts
  • survival_rate — agents alive at episode end
  • distance_between_agents — spatial cohesion
